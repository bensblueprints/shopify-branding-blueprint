Shopify Branding Blueprint

Pre-Launch Checklist

Day 7: Everything You Need Before Going Live

Instructions: Go through each item and check it off when complete. Don't launch until you've completed all critical (red) items. Orange items are recommended, green items are nice-to-have.

Brand Foundation

  • Brand name finalized and domain secured
    Make sure domain matches brand name, check trademark availability
  • Logo and brand assets completed
    Main logo, favicon, social media profile images
  • Brand colors and fonts defined
    Primary, secondary colors + heading and body fonts
  • Brand story written
    About page content ready

Products & Inventory

  • Products sourced and quality tested
    Ordered samples, verified quality meets standards
  • Pricing strategy finalized
    Prices set with healthy margins (aim for 60%+)
  • Product photos and descriptions ready
    High-quality photos, compelling benefit-focused descriptions
  • Inventory or supplier ready to fulfill
    Stock on hand or supplier can ship within promised timeframe

Store Setup

  • Shopify store setup complete
    Theme customized, navigation working, mobile-responsive
  • Payment processing configured
    Shopify Payments or alternative provider active and tested
  • Shipping rates and zones configured
    Accurate shipping costs, delivery timeframes displayed
  • Test order placed and fulfilled
    Complete checkout, receive order confirmation, test fulfillment

Legal & Policies

  • Privacy Policy published
    Required by law, covers data collection and usage
  • Terms of Service published
    Legal terms for using your store
  • Refund/Return Policy published
    Clear policy on returns, refunds, exchanges
  • Contact information visible
    Email, contact form, or phone number accessible

Marketing Setup

  • Email marketing platform connected
    Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or similar integrated with store
  • Welcome email sequence ready
    Automated emails for new subscribers
  • Social media profiles created
    Instagram, TikTok, Facebook with consistent branding
  • Facebook Pixel / Analytics installed
    Tracking in place before any traffic arrives

Final Checks

  • Mobile experience tested
    All pages look and function well on mobile devices
  • All links working
    No broken links, all buttons and CTAs functional
  • Page speed optimized
    Site loads in under 3 seconds
  • Friend/family review complete
    Fresh eyes checked for typos, confusing elements
