Brand Story Template

Day 3: Crafting Your Brand Story

Instructions: A great brand story has three parts: the problem, the transformation, and the mission. Use this template to craft your compelling brand narrative that connects emotionally with customers.

The Brand Story Framework

Part 1: The Problem (The World Before)

What frustration or gap in the market inspired your brand? What were you or your customers experiencing?

Part 2: The Transformation (The Discovery)

What insight or moment led to creating this brand? What makes your approach different?

Part 3: The Mission (The Future)

What is your brand's purpose? What do you want to help customers achieve or become?
