# Shopify Branding Blueprint - 30 Lesson Thumbnail Prompts for Gemini

## Course Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Colors:** Deep navy blue, gold accents (#C9A962), dark backgrounds
- **Style:** Premium, professional, minimalist, modern
- **Target:** Entrepreneurs building Shopify brands

## Usage Instructions for Gemini

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)
2. Copy a thumbnail prompt from above
3. Paste it into Gemini
4. Download the generated image
5. Optionally refine with follow-up prompts like:
   - "Make the text larger and bolder"
   - "Add more contrast between the background and foreground"
   - "Make it more dramatic/premium/minimal"

**Total Course Duration:** ~404 minutes (6.7 hours)
